About Me

I graduated in 2016 from Vassar College with a major in Economics and a minor in Mathematics. During my undegrad, I worked for two-years as a research assistant for Prof. Benjamin Ho (Vassar College). Between 2016-2017, I worked for Prof. Katherine Milkman (Wharton) and Prof. John Beshears (HBS). Since 2017, I have been working for Prof. Alex Rees-Jones (Wharton) and Prof. Benjamin Lockwood (Wharton).

Currently, my interests lie on the intersection between behavioral and labor economics. However, I haven't even begun grad school, so don't hold me to it!

Eager to start my Ph.D. and begin my academic career!!
